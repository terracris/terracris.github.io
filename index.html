What is my
favorite project?

Great question! That is a difficult question to answer. I have loved each one of my projects. Each one has taught me a great deal of how to be a stronger person, creator, and do-er. If I had to pick one I would choose my senior thesis project, or Major Qualifying Project (MQP) as WPI likes to call them. I love the creative freedom and experimentation to develop solutions to problems that arise. Additionally, I love my team dynamic. We deligate tasks and trust that we will complete our respective tasks with exceptional quality. When we need help, we are comfortable with requesting assistance.
