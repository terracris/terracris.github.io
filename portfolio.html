Unified Robotics IV: Navigation

In this project, our main goal was to create a ROS architecture enabling a Turtlebot to autonomously map, localize, and navigate through an unknown maze using a LiDAR. The specific tasks included self-localization within the maze, obstacle avoidance during maze traversal, identification of exploration frontiers for navigation, mapping the entire maze, and the ability to navigate back to any point on the map.
